『Chapter 1』Dudley Demented

A. Vocabulary, Collocations & idioms

  • How much longer was he supposed to endure Sirius telling him to sit tight and be a good boy?

    • 听话（将自己置身事外？）

  • And his reward was to be stuck in Little Winging for four solid weeks.

    • 连续四周（不愉快的）

  • Those furious thoughts whirled around in Harry’s head, and his inside writhed with anger as a sultry, velvety night fell around him.

    • 火冒三丈

  • a hardened hooligan

    • 顽固不化的街头混混

  • He has grown so accustomed to venting his frustration on his family that he didn’t even realize he was doing it.

    • 拿别人当出气筒

  • Harry mastered the impulse to call after them. After, seeking a fight is not a smart move.

    • 控制住了冲动

  • He could hear fourteen years’ hatred of Dudley pounding in his vein.

    • 愤怒在他的血管中躁动

  • Dudley gave an odd, shuddering gasp, as though he had been doused in icy water.

    • 猛地一激灵、倒吸一口冷气，好像突然浸入冰水

  • He opened his eyes to their full extent.

    • 瞪大了眼睛

  • Mrs. Figg, their batty old neighbor, came panting into sight.

    • 渐渐进入视野

C. Encyclopedia of Wizarding World

  • Address of the Dursleys:
    • No. 4, Privet Drive, Little Whinging, Surrey
  • Depiction of Dementors:
    • A towering, hooded figure was gliding smoothly towards him, hovering over the ground, no feet or face visible beneath its robes, sucking on the night as it came.
    • Harry could feel their putrid, death-cold breath filling his own lungs.
  • Depiction of Patronous Charm:
    • A silvery wisp of vapor shot from the tip of his wand.
    • A enormous silver stag erupted from the tip of Harry’s wand; its antlers caught dementors in the place where the heart should have been.

D. Beautifully written sentences

  • Harry preferred Little Whinging by night, when the curtained windows made patches of jewel-bright color in the darkness and he ran no danger of hearing disapproving mutters about his ‘delinquent’ appearance when he passed the householders. (P11)
